Sentiments/Emotions

Currently our system support following types of moods/tone.
These are generated or detected by using the IBM Watson API.

ANGER

Anger is evoked due to injustice, conflict, negligence, or betrayal. If anger is active, the individual attacks the target, verbally or physically.

JOY

Joy (or happiness) has shades of enjoyment, satisfaction, and pleasure. Joy brings a sense of well-being, inner peace, love, safety, and contentment.

SADNESS

Sadness indicates a feeling of loss and disadvantage. When a person is quiet, less energetic, and withdrawn, it can be inferred that they feel sadness.

FEAR

Fear is a response to impending danger. It is a survival mechanism that is triggered as a reaction to some negative stimulus.
